1 /** @defgroup tcmi_syscall_group syscall forwarding
2  *
3  * @ingroup tcmi_module_class
4  *
5  * @brief Classes in this group are used for system calls modification and forwarding to provide migration transparency.
6  *
7  * Each class is divided into parts with related system calls:
8  *
9  * - \b PID \b \& \b GID \b manipulation - These methods are used for PID and GID
10  * manipulation or works with sessions
11  *
12  * - \b User \b identification - Methods from this part works with user identification of current process
13  *
14  * - \b Group \b identification - Methods from this part works with group identification of current process
15  *
16  * - \b Signal \b sending - These methods are used for sending signals
17  *
18  * - \b Generic - Methods from this part are able to handle more calls from diferent groups. Only tcmi_guest_rpc has
19  * this part.
20  *
